My YouTube Channel

I made a YouTube channel a few months ago, and I am in need of subscribers. It would be great to make it to 1000 subscribers, and every subscriber counts.

When you hit 1000 subscribers, YouTube reviews your channel, and then if everything is good, (no songs/movies/pictures without copyright info) they will approve your channel, and then ads will show up on your videos, and when they are clicked on or watched completely, you get paid about 15-25 cents per ad. You want as many subscribers as you can get, to maximize ad watches/clicks. You get banned if you click ads on your own channel

My channel is mostly about unicycles and Ripstiks, but overall it is a vlog channel.
